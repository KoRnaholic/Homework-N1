Some information about cats

Cats, domesticated companions for centuries, belong to the family
Felidae. Known for their agility and independence,
they come in various breeds, sizes, and colors. Felis catus,
their scientific name, are carnivorous mammals with retractable claws.
Cats communicate through meows, purrs, and body language,
fostering diverse relationships with humans
